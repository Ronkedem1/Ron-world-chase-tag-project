World Chase Tag - History and Records

History of World Chase Tag

World Chase Tag (WCT) was founded in 2011 by brothers Christian and Damien Devaux, combining the childhood game of tag with parkour and obstacle course elements. Over the years, it has grown into an international phenomenon, attracting athletes and fans from around the world. WCT has introduced unique gameplay rules and a signature tag arena, known as "The Quad."

From its humble beginnings, WCT has hosted numerous championships, innovating the way people view competitive tag as both a sport and an art form.
